# ❓ FAQ

### Is VeriMeZK free to use?

Yes — it’s open-source and MIT licensed.

---

### Does it store any personal data?

No. All processing is local. Nothing is stored or sent.

---

### Can I use it without a blockchain?

Yes. Proofs can be verified off-chain too.

---

### What if the face scan fails?

The user will be prompted to retry. Good lighting and camera quality help.

---

### Can I define my own rules?

Absolutely! See [Custom Checks](./features/custom-checks.md).
